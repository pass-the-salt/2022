The conference is dedicated to Free Software (+ open protocols/formats) and Security. It is totally free! All talks are in English.


Conferences

Talks will be about Free Software and Security. All the talks shall be given in English. The 2022 edition will host 26 talks, grouped in 8 sessions.

Workshops

This edition hosts 5 workshops on Reverse, Network, Theat intel and NFC topics. Come to discover and practice!

Networking

Exchange experiences and create new relationships with open minded attendees or speakers in a warm and open-minded ambiance.
